our donate plan

Our next plan is to help them build their homes, solve the health issues as well as take the necessary action after the disaster

food for a family

We will provide healthy balanced food according to the quantity, among them Rice, Powder milk, Egg, Dal, Sault, Drinking water and so on.

medicine for a family

In this circumstances, health issues are a major concern for us. People are now isolated from their nearest hospital. Especially children or pregnant women who are suffering need treatment, but are not getting it. Our plane is to reach there and provide them better treatment and neccessary medicine.

shelter for a family

Almost all of them have no shelter, especially in rural areas. They are spending day and night without a roof. Which is very difficult for them to accept, even if their crops have been washed away by the flood waters. Our plan is to help them build their homes and buy them some neccessary furnitures and household.
